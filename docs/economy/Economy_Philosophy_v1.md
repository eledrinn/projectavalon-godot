# Economy Philosophy v1

**Status:** LOCKED — Auction house confirmed, framework established

## Purpose
This document establishes the locked philosophical foundation for the game's economy, trade, and item systems.

---

## Locked Decisions

### Auction House: YES
- **Status:** LOCKED
- **Implementation:** Full auction house exists
- **Scope:** Available in 1–10 vertical slice (city hub at minimum)
- **Design notes:** Creates player-driven economy, price discovery, trade social dynamics

### Party Size: 4 PLAYERS
- **Status:** LOCKED
- **Applies to:** Group content, encounter design, party composition
- **Rationale:** Small enough for coordination, large enough for role variety

### Mounts: LATE UNLOCK
- **Status:** LOCKED
- **Mount timing:** Beyond level 10 (post-vertical slice or very late in slice)
- **Early alternative:** Glider questline (traversing high terrain)
- **Glider design:** Long-ish questline rewards first glider; allows descent/traversal from elevation
- **Philosophy:** World feels large early; mounts are meaningful when earned

### Level 1–10 Pacing: ~4–6 HOURS
- **Status:** LOCKED
- **Target:** 4–6 hours for experienced MMO players; 6–8+ hours for new players
- **Philosophy:** Substantial journey, not a tutorial rush
- **End point:** Entering convergence city with sense of accomplishment

---

## Trade Philosophy (LOCKED)

### Player Trade
- **Auction House:** Enabled (primary trade method)
- **Direct Trade:** Enabled (player-to-player)
- **Vendors:** Exist for basic goods, repairs, consumables
- **Balance:** Player economy > vendor economy for gear and valuables

### Regional Resource Identity
- **Status:** TENTATIVE direction
- **Principle:** Resources have regional identity (where you gather matters)
- **Effect:** Encourages travel across seamless overworld
- **Examples:**
  - Wet basin: Fungal reagents, reed fibers
  - Mountain regions: Ore, stone, relic beast materials
  - Ash frontier: Volcanic minerals, alchemical components

### Exploration and Gathering
- **Status:** TENTATIVE direction
- **Principle:** Gathering encourages exploration and discovery
- **Implementation:** Rare materials in hidden/remote locations
- **Risk/Reward:** Dangerous areas = better materials

---

## Crafting Philosophy (TENTATIVE)

### Vertical Slice (1–10) Scope
- **Crafting role:** Light — consumables and basic sidegrades only
- **Professions:** Limited (possibly one gathering profession: Survival/Scavenging)
- **Advanced crafting:** Deferred beyond 1–10

### Class Integration
- Crafting supports class progression where possible
- Rare materials for ritual/learning cards
- Not required for core progression

---

## Itemization Philosophy (LOCKED)

See `/Docs/GameDesign/Progression_Philosophy_v0.md` for full details.

**Summary:**
- Gear and class progression have equal weight
- Set bonuses change gameplay (not just stats)
- Legendary items enable new builds
- Sidegrades exist at all tiers

---

## Deferred Beyond Vertical Slice

- Advanced crafting professions (blacksmithing, tailoring, etc.)
- Guild/corporate economic structures
- Cross-region trade routes (requires multiple regions)
- Player housing/economy tie-ins
- Mount-based trade caravans
