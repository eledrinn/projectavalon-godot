# Regional Resource Map v0

## Stillwater Village
*Coastal settlement at the edge of the redwood-like forest*

**Gathering Nodes**
- Tidepools: shellfish, kelp, salt deposits
- Coastal driftwood: weathered hardwood, resin pockets
- Mist gardens: moisture-loving herbs, lichens

**Crafting Stations**
- Saltery: food preservation, salt extraction
- Net-weaving post: basic cloth, rope, fishing gear

**Vendor Specialties**
- Fishmonger: preserved seafood, bait, waterproofing oils
- Driftwood artisan: rustic furniture, carved charms

---

## Miregarden
*Inland wet fungal basin — native homeland region*

**Gathering Nodes**
- Fungal groves: medicinal mushrooms, spore clusters, luminescent caps
- peat bogs: compressed organics, acidic tannins, bog iron
- Reed beds: thatching materials, fibrous stalks, waterfowl

**Crafting Stations**
- Fermentation pit: preserved foods, brewing, organic reagents
- Weaving loom: reed cloth, fungal leather, rope

**Vendor Specialties**
- Fungal cultivator: rare mushrooms, spore cultures, luminescent dyes
- Tradition keeper: native crafts, ritual items, oral knowledge (quest hooks)

---

## Ashroot Thicket
*Foothill transition zone, first signs of volcanic influence*

**Gathering Nodes**
- Charred groves: fire-resistant bark, ash-enriched soil samples
- Hot springs: mineral deposits, sulfurous compounds, therapeutic muds
- Transition zone: hybrid flora (wetland + upland species)

**Crafting Stations**
- Char station: charcoal production, pyro-processing
- Mineral wash: basic ore refinement, chemical separation

**Vendor Specialties**
- Ash-harvesters: volcanic soil amendments, fire-starting materials
- Transition traders: goods from both lowland and upland sources

---

## Gaspocket Flats
*Sulfuric valley with visible volcanic activity*

**Gathering Nodes**
- Fumarole fields: sulfur deposits, volcanic gases (requires protection)
- Obsidian outcrops: volcanic glass, sharp fragments
- Heat-adapted flora: thermophilic organisms, heat-resistant fibers

**Crafting Stations**
- Crucible furnace: glassworking, metal alloying, high-heat processing
- Gas capture rig: reagent collection, experimental materials

**Vendor Specialties**
- Crucible master: glassware, alloyed metals, experimental tools
- Gas-runner: hazardous material transport, reagent supply (danger premium)

---

## Terrace Foot
*First convergence city base — where cultures meet*

**Gathering Nodes**
- Trade route intersections: varied goods from all directions
- Construction quarries: building stone, aggregate, clay
- Urban forage: food waste recovery, urban woodlots

**Crafting Stations**
- Assembly yard: construction materials, large-item fabrication
- Appraisal table: item evaluation, certification, quality grading

**Vendor Specialties**
- Route broker: courier contracts, travel permits, route information
- Exchange factor: currency conversion, bulk trading, warehousing
- Convergence merchant: curated selection from all regional sources
