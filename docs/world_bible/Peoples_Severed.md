# Peoples: Severed v0

## Status
Locked direction, with room for internal subtypes and naming later.

## Core Identity
The Severed are a survival-engineered offshoot of the Arrivals.

They share the same sacred-classical roots, but centuries of catastrophe, secrecy, adaptation, and bitterness turned their traditions into something harsher:
- practical
- reinforced
- pressure-based
- industrial in a fantasy sense
- suspicious of old dependence on power

They are not generic steampunk.
They are not sci-fi.
They are not whimsical tinkerers.

They are engineered survivors.

## Emotional Read
They should feel:
- severe
- disciplined
- bitter
- pragmatic
- ascetic
- concealed
- efficient
- old but changed

They should not feel:
- goofy inventors
- Victorian steampunk cosplay
- cartoon evil tech people
- modern industrialists
- lightning-powered mage engineers

## Physical Identity
The Severed should remain recognizably related to the Arrivals, but visibly altered by:
- harsh environments
- engineered survival
- decades/centuries of masking, pressure systems, and adaptation
- a different posture and emotional center

Compared to Arrivals, they should feel:
- harder
- more enclosed
- more burdened
- more reinforced
- less open and luminous

## Surface / Gear Logic
The Severed visual identity should come more from gear and presentation than biology.

Strong elements:
- masks
- wraps
- layered protective wear
- reinforced gloves and boots
- riveted plates
- pressure seals
- harnesses
- cable rigs
- ash-resistant hoods
- alchemical canisters or containers
- utilitarian eye pieces if needed, but not cliché goggles everywhere

## Material Language
Use:
- bronze
- dark metal
- heat-treated fittings
- rivets
- leather
- ash-stained fabrics
- reinforced wood
- heavy rope
- chain
- pressure tanks or housings
- valves and vents in moderation
- carved old Arrival materials repurposed into harder structures

Avoid:
- electricity aesthetics
- tesla-coil nonsense
- whimsical gear spam
- too many exposed cogs for style alone

## Technology Style
The Severed use fantasy-industrial technology based on:
- steam
- pressure
- cable
- counterweight
- geothermal heat
- alchemical fuel
- ballast
- pulleys
- rope tows
- cable cars
- cliffside lifts
- extraction rigs
- airships

Their geometry remains, but as engineering doctrine rather than sacred order.

## Architecture Language
Severed built spaces should feel:
- cliff-cut
- fortified
- suspended
- repurposed
- efficient
- heat-scarred
- vertical

Think:
- old Arrival foundations converted into extraction sites
- cable-linked towers
- suspended platforms
- furnace chambers
- pressure houses
- sealed work yards
- narrow bridges and industrial perches
- anchored cranes and lifts over dangerous terrain

They do not build prettily.
They build to survive, extract, and control.

## Airships
Airships are part of their visual identity, but should stay fantasy-grounded.

Use:
- ribbed frames
- treated cloth or membrane
- ballast systems
- suspended cages or cargo frames
- pressure/furnace logic
- cliff docking and practical transport

They should feel ominous and functional, not luxurious.

## Relationship to Other Peoples
### Toward Arrivals
The Arrivals represent:
- old failure
- returning doctrine
- judgment
- the possible restart of the old cycle

### Toward Natives
The Severed may see natives as:
- unpredictable
- too bound to taboo
- obstacles
- populations to manage or manipulate
- witnesses to old wounds

They are not trying to be liked. They are trying to secure and survive.

## Mythic Position
Among the Natives, the Severed survive in fractured memory as:
- protectors
- false gods
- betrayers
- oathbreakers
- tragic elder figures

This should make their reveal more potent.

## Visual Summary
The Severed are harsh fantasy-industrial descendants of the Arrivals, defined by:
- reinforced silhouettes
- masks and pressure gear
- cable, steam, rope, ballast, and furnace logic
- cliffside and vertical extraction architecture
- geometry transformed from holiness into engineering discipline

## Avoid
- generic steampunk clichés
- electricity-based aesthetics
- silly gadget overload
- “evil empire in brass”
- losing the sense that they come from the same roots as the Arrivals
