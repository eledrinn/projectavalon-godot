# Crafting and Gathering — Vertical Slice v1

**Status:** LOCKED — Basic crafting for 1–10: Mining, Herblore, Clothier

## Philosophy (LOCKED)

### 1–10 Scope
- **Basic crafting only:** Consumables, basic gear sidegrades
- **Not a focus:** Crafting supports gameplay, doesn't define it
- **Hand-in-hand with gathering:** Raw materials come from gathering professions
- **Equal weight with drops:** Crafted items = world drops, not better or worse

### Post-10 / Full Game
- Expanded professions
- Specialization trees
- Rare recipes, legendary crafting
- Guild/corporate crafting structures

---

## Gathering Professions (LOCKED for 1–10)

### 1. Mining
**Gathers:**
- Ore (copper, tin, iron equivalents)
- Stone, minerals
- Volcanic minerals (ash frontier)
- Gemstones (rare)

**Used for:** Blacksmithing (ore), various crafting (stone, gems)

**Nodes:**
- Rocky outcrops, cave walls, cliff faces
- Mountain regions, foothills
- Ash frontier (unique volcanic ores)

---

### 2. Herblore
**Gathers:**
- Medicinal herbs (healing potions)
- Alkaloid herbs (buff potions)
- **Psychoactive compounds** (unique to Avalon — vision quests, ritual components, temporary perception changes)
- Fungal growths (wet basin specialty)

**Used for:** Alchemy (potions), ritual components for Cards

**Nodes:**
- Wet basin (fungal shelves, marsh edges)
- Forest understory (redwood belt)
- Ash frontier (resilient/fire-adapted species)
- Relic creature dens (rare psychoactives)

**Psychoactive compounds note:**
- Temporary visual/perception changes (see hidden paths, spirit world glimpses)
- Ritual use for unlocking certain Cards
- Not "drugs" — mystical, cultural, world-integrated
- Examples:
  - "Spore-touched vision" — see hidden fungal paths
  - "Ash-rite clarity" — temporarily sense Severed machinery
  - "Basin trance" — communication with Bonder Aspects

---

### 3. Salvage (Clothier source)
**Not a gathering profession in traditional sense:**
- **Source:** Enemy drops (cloth scraps, leather hides, bone, sinew)
- **Alternative gathering:** Rare "relic beast" harvesting (Bonder ritual)

**Materials:**
- Cloth scraps (humanoid enemies)
- Leather hides (beasts)
- Bone, sinew, feathers (relic creatures)
- Chitin (insects, crustaceans)

**Used for:** Clothier (armor, bags)

---

## Crafting Professions (LOCKED for 1–10)

### 1. Blacksmithing
**Uses:** Mining (ore, stone)

**Creates:**
- Weapons (basic, sidegrade quality)
- Armor (metal types)
- Tools (mining picks, skinning knives)
- Repair items

**1–10 scope:**
- Basic recipes only
- No specializations
- Equal to world drops (not better)

---

### 2. Alchemy
**Uses:** Herblore (medicinals, alkaloids, psychoactives)

**Creates:**
- Healing potions (instant, HoT)
- Buff potions (stats, resistances)
- **Vision/ritual potions** (psychoactive — required for some Card unlocks)
- Poisons (for Armsman/Ranger)
- Dyes, cosmetic effects

**1–10 scope:**
- Basic potions only
- One ritual potion type (Basin trance equivalent)

---

### 3. Clothier
**Uses:** Salvage (cloth, leather, bone, sinew from enemies)

**Creates:**
- Cloth armor (Natives, casters)
- Leather armor (Rangers, Bonders)
- Bags, containers
- Ritual garments (cosmetic + minor buff)

**1–10 scope:**
- Light/medium armor only
- No heavy armor (Blacksmithing only for metal)

## Profession Mechanics (TENTATIVE)

### Learning Recipes
- Vendor trainers (basic recipes)
- World discovery (rare recipes in hidden locations)
- Quest rewards (unique recipes)

### Skill-ups
- Classic crafting: craft items to skill up
- Orange/Yellow/Green/Grey difficulty tiers
- 1–10 cap: 100 skill points (example)

### Tools
- Mining: Pickaxe (equipped or inventory)
- Herblore: Sickle/basket
- Clothier: Needle/thread (cosmetic only, not consumed)

---

## Integration with Progression

### Cards
- Some Cards require crafted/ritual items to unlock
- Example: Bonder Aspect unlock may require specific alchemy potion

### Economy
- Gathered materials sellable on AH
- Crafted items compete with world drops
- Rare recipes have value

---

## 1–10 Progression

### Early (1–3)
- Basic gathering unlocked
- Vendor recipes only
- Simple potions, basic armor

### Mid (4–7)
- World recipes discovered
- First psychoactive potions (ritual use)
- Bags unlocked

### Late (8–10)
- Rare recipes from dungeons/quests
- First set bonus gear (crafted)
- Ritual items for post-10 Card unlocks

---

## Deferred Beyond 1–10

- Inscription (Chants for Forceshapers)
