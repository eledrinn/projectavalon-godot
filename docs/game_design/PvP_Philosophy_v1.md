# PvP Philosophy v1

**Status:** LOCKED — PvP exists long-term; one battleground for vertical slice

## Philosophy (LOCKED)

### Long-Term Vision
**PvP should exist and be balanced.** It is not an afterthought.

### Design Goals
- **Optional but meaningful:** PvP rewards should matter, but not be required for PvE
- **Skill-based over gear-based:** Good play beats gear differences
- **World-integrated:** PvP has lore justification (faction tensions, Severed incursions)
- **Multiple formats:** Battlegrounds, open-world objectives, possible arena later

---

## PvP in 1–10 Vertical Slice (LOCKED)

### Scope: One Battleground
**One PvP battleground included in the 1–10 vertical slice.**

### Purpose
- Demonstrates PvP philosophy early
- Tests class balance in competitive environment
- Provides end-game activity for demo/vertical slice
- Gathers feedback for long-term tuning

### Battleground Design (TENTATIVE)
**Size:** 4v4 (matches party size) or 8v8 (two parties)

**Theme:** Faction skirmish or resource control
- **Option A:** Native vs Arrival territorial dispute (lore-justified)
- **Option B:** Severed incursion repelled by combined forces (PvE/PvP hybrid)
- **Option C:** Relic site control (neutral objective)

**Mechanics (classic):**
- Capture the flag
- King of the hill (resource/control points)
- Deathmatch variant

**Duration:** 10–15 minutes

**Rewards (1–10 scope):**
- Honor points (track for future rewards)
- Unique cosmetic (tabard, trophy)
- One PvP-specific item (sidegrade, not best-in-slot)

## Class Balance in PvP (TENTATIVE)

### Philosophy
All 6 classes should be viable in PvP.

### Expected Roles
| Class | PvP Role | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Apogist** | Control/Support | Gravity effects, constellation finishers |
| **Tephrate** | Tank/Bruiser | Pressure builds, disruption |
| **Bonder** | Versatile/Striker | Aspect switching for different matchups |
| **Forceshaper** | Control/Damage | Elemental zoning, storm burst |
| **Armsman** | Bruiser/Duelist | Openings exploitation |
| **Ranger** | Ranged/Damage | Line control, kiting |

### Tuning Philosophy
- **CC (crowd control):** Diminishing returns, not chain-CC
- **Healing:** Viable but not infinite (mana/pressure limits)
- **Time-to-kill:** 15–30 seconds (not one-shot, not eternal)
- **Burst vs sustained:** Both viable builds

## PvP vs PvE Balance

### Gear Equality (TENTATIVE)
**Option A:** PvP gear scales to standardized level (equalized stats)
- Pros: Skill-based, no gear grind for PvP
- Cons: Less reward for PvE investment

**Option B:** PvP gear separate track, comparable power to PvE
- Pros: Clear progression for PvP-focused players
- Cons: Gear disparity possible

**Option C:** Hybrid — base stats equalized, small gear bonuses
- Compromise position

**Decision:** TBD later, not required for 1–10 battleground
