# Fast Travel and Public Transport v1

**Status:** LOCKED — Fast travel restricted, public transport rare

## Fast Travel Philosophy (LOCKED)

### Principle
**The world should be traversed.** Fast travel is the exception, not the rule.

### Restricted Fast Travel
- **Not generally available** to all players
- **Class-specific abilities only:** 1–2 classes may have limited teleport/summon mechanics
- **Examples from classic WoW:**
  - Mages: Teleport to major locations (self only)
  - Warlocks: Summon party members (requires multiple people, reagent)
- **Avalon equivalent:** Possibly Forceshaper (wind/teleport) and/or Tephrate (pressure rifts) — TBD by Systems Designer

### Design Goals
- Preserve world scale and journey feel
- Make fast travel a **strategic choice**, not a convenience
- Require preparation, resources, or group coordination
- Class identity moment: "We need a [Class] to get us there"

## Public Transport (LOCKED)

### Principle
**Rare, scheduled, immersive transport** between major locations.

### Transport Types

| Type | Description | Availability |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Boats** | Coastal/river travel between ports | Rare routes, scheduled departures |
| **Airships** | Severed-style pressure vessels, cliff-to-cliff | Late game, faction-based |
| **Creative TBD** | Native reed-barges? Arrival celestial transit? | Design later, keep rare |

### Design Rules
- **Rare:** Not every zone connected; only major hubs
- **Scheduled:** Departures at set times (e.g., every 15 minutes), not on-demand
- **Immersive:** You board, wait, travel, disembark — not a loading screen
- **Seamless:** Travel happens in real-time (you can walk around the boat, look out)
- **Optional:** Walking is always an alternative

### 1–10 Vertical Slice
- **Likely:** One boat route (coastal between Arrival landing and Native basin)
- **Unlock:** Mid-to-late 1–10, after glider questline
- **Purpose:** Demonstrates public transport philosophy without overbuilding

## Shrine / Binding System

### Bind Points
- Players bind to shrines (camps for Arrivals, sacred sites for Natives, pressure anchors for Severed)
- **Death respawn:** Return to bound shrine
- **Fast travel:** NO — binding is for death only, not teleport

### Potential Exception (TENTATIVE)
- Late-game "Hearthstone" equivalent (long cooldown, single location)
- If implemented: Long cooldown (1+ hour), single personal location, interrupted by combat
- **1–10:** Not available

## Comparison to Classic MMOs

| Feature | WoW Classic | Avalon |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Fast travel | Flight paths (slow), mage portals, hearthstone | Class-specific only (1–2 classes) |
| Public transport | Zeppelins, boats (rare, scheduled) | Boats, airships (rare, scheduled, seamless) |
| Death travel | Spirit healers | Shrine respawn + corpse run |
| World feel | Large, traversal matters | Larger early, gliders add verticality |
