# Classes Overview v0

## Status
Core class framework is locked at a high level. Specs, abilities, and numbers remain open.

## Class Design Rules
Every class must have:
- one strong fantasy
- one strong gameplay signature
- one progression flavor
- clear role expression
- readable visual identity
- compatibility with both major cultural/player backgrounds where applicable

The classes should feel world-integrated rather than pasted on.

## Current Roster
- Apogist
- Tephrate
- Bonder
- Forceshaper
- Armsman
- Ranger

---

## Apogist
### Core Fantasy
A celestial and gravitic class dealing in stars, alignment, tranquility, gravity, tides, pressure, and collapse.

### Cultural Split
- Ordered: stars, harmony, calm, precision, healing
- Primordial: gravity, pressure, tidal force, collapse, cataclysmic force

### Signature Mechanic
Constellation finisher gameplay.
The Apogist builds toward a finisher window and completes a high-skill celestial pattern.

### Progression Flavor
Constellations

As Apogists advance, they discover rarer constellations tied to the world’s skies and deeper celestial archetypes.

### Roles
- damage
- healing
(possible control/support expression through design)

---

## Tephrate
### Core Fantasy
A dark volcanic and taboo class touching ancient feared power associated with ash, ember, volcanic force, containment, rupture, and the world’s old wound.

### Signature Mechanic
Pressure / Vent / Faults

The Tephrate builds Pressure, creates Faults, and vents them for powerful tanking or damage effects.

### Progression Flavor
Tongues

The Tephrate uncovers and speaks forbidden Tongues of power tied to old feared truths.

### Role Lanes
- stone-oriented tank/control
- ash/fire-oriented damage

### Tone
Should feel infernal-adjacent and feared without becoming generic demon magic.

---

## Bonder
### Core Fantasy
A class of living pacts, instinct, and inherited aspects tied to surviving pre-Ash lineages of wildlife.

The Bonder does not simply shapeshift randomly.
The Bonder bonds with ancient surviving lineages and channels their aspects.

### Signature Mechanic
Instinct / Call-and-Answer / Aspect Visions

The Bonder builds Instinct, invokes a bonded Aspect, and completes a short in-combat bond response.
During this, the player experiences a brief vision or manifestation of the bonded being.

### Progression Flavor
Aspects

The Bonder unlocks Aspects through ritual, worthiness, class quests, tracking, and encounters with living relic lineages.

### Starter Aspect Roles
- tank = horned mountain survivor lineage
- damage = aerial relic predator lineage
- healing = giant-sloth-like survivor lineage

### Tone
Ancient, relational, reverent, wild but not feral-chaotic.

---

## Forceshaper
### Core Fantasy
A formal elemental discipline with focus on wind, water, storm, and weather-like forces.

This is the elemental class brought by the Arrivals and taught in formalized ways, later adapted by native learners.

### Signature Mechanic
Confluence

The Forceshaper sequences elemental currents in meaningful order for enhanced effects.

### Progression Flavor
Chants

Formal Chants encode techniques and manipulations of elemental force.

### Key Role Directions
- wind = mobility / utility damage
- water = healing / cleansing / renewal / fungal/rot-adjacent life logic
- storm = burst / charged damage

### Tone
Disciplined, flowing, formal, and more atmospheric than purely destructive.

---

## Armsman
### Core Fantasy
The broad martial generalist. Covers fighter, duelist, weapon master, and some rogue-like practical combat expression without splitting into a separate thief archetype yet.

### Signature Mechanic
Openings

The Armsman creates and exploits enemy weaknesses through timing, positioning, and technique.

### Progression Flavor
Etchings

Weapon or combat Etchings provide martial identity, recipe-like progression, and philosophy without turning the class into a caster.

### Role Directions
- tank/control lane
- heavier weapon damage lane
- lighter, more mobile/skirmish lane

### Tone
Grounded, martial, versatile, disciplined.

---

## Ranger
### Core Fantasy
The grounded ranged hunter/archer/scout class.

### Signature Mechanic
Read the Line

The Ranger identifies, predicts, or prepares a clean line of attack and capitalizes on it.

### Progression Flavor
Strings

Strings represent shot philosophy, discipline, draw/release mastery, and class identity without overcomplicating the class into ammo micromanagement.

### Tone
Patient, aware, exact, practical.

### Role Direction
Primarily ranged damage and scouting/control utility.

---

## Cross-Class Design Rule
These classes should not feel culturally locked in the shallow sense.
The world gives them different expressions, but the gameplay chassis must remain clear and readable.

## Future Notes
Possible future expansion may add:
- one more native player race
- one more arrival player race
- deeper class progression expressions
- more role expansion within the classes already established
